I’m a PhD student at the University of Copenhagen. I’m mainly interested in galaxy formation and evolution, and tend to approach this subject through a combination of observational and theoretical techniques. I’m a heavy user of the JWST data, and in particular the NIRCam and NIRSpec instruments. Notably, I’ve compiled the largest uniformly reduced sample of NIRSpec prism spectra. I analyse this data using a spectral energy distribution (SED) fitting code of my own design (genesis), optimised to fit the NIRSpec prism spectra. I model the inferred galaxy properties with analytical and semi-analytical models, with a focus on understanding the merger, star formation, chemical enrichment, dust production, and feedback histories of early universe galaxies.
